About

A web picross game! All of the puzzles are hand-made, so take your time and solve!
If you make a mistake, it will not mark a mistake. It will only tell you if the whole puzzle is solved or not.

How to Play

Left click on a cell to fill it in!
Right click to mark it with an X.

The goal of picross is to fill in the cells so that they fit the clues given by the numbers around the grid. Each number tells you how many filled cells there are next to each other. If there's more than 1 number, there needs to be at least one empty cell until there's another filled cell.

Order matters! The order of numbers tells you what order the filled cells appear in the grid. If a number is to the left of another in the same row, then those filled cells will appear before the other ones.
