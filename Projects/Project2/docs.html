Cool Technical Stuff

With this project, the 'controls' aren't actually used on the search function. While searching is one of the controls on the website, the main controls are actually found on the monsters themselves.

When you add a monster to your combat queue, it has it's base HP and an empty initiative field. Users can fill these in with their own information and it will update in both of the places that the monster exists on the site. (the combat queue and the monster information section)

I spent a lot of extra effort trying to make the controls update the entire site, and not just the place where you changed it.

Yes, whenever monster information is changed, almost all of the elements are swapped out. I was just too used to draw() calls to have it work a different way =P.
